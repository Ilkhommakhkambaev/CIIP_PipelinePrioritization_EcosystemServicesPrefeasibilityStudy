Objective
Design and apply a methodology to identify and rank a GCF-ready national project pipeline.

Climate project ideas are scattered across ministries in different formats with no system to evaluate which ones are actually ready for GCF funding. Without a structured pipeline, Tajikistan cannot tell GCF what it wants to fund next.

Ecosystem Services
Prefeasibility

Objective
Test whether the ecosystem services concept is viable and give CEP a clear pathway to a GCF Concept Note.
Why
The Readiness proposal identified ecosystem services (pastures, restoration, eco-tourism) as high potential for Pamir and Khujand, but there is no evidence yet that it works technically, financially, or institutionally. Before CEP can ask GCF for millions, someone has to check if the idea holds together.
